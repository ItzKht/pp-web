The Day We Spoke

The day we spoke for the first time… vo din yaad hai mujhe, jab hum pehli baar saamne saamne mile the. Shayad baatein zyada nahi hui thi, but tumhara vo smile, vo aankhon ka shine, sab kuch dil ko chhoo gaya tha. Us din laga tha ki kitni simple, kitni sweet ho tum. Bas dekhta raha tha tumhe… shayad tabhi nahi samjha tha, par aaj realise hota hai, vo pehli mulaqat hi dil me bas gayi thi. Phir next day, jab tum Khushi ke saath call pe thi, aur tum piche baithi thi, tumhari awaaz sunke ajeeb sa sukoon mila tha. Vo choti choti baatein… tumhara thoda sharmaana, thoda muskuraana, sab kuch dil ko itna acha lag raha tha. I didn’t know us din ki vo choti si call, aur pehli mulaqat… meri life ka itna important part ban jaayegi. Tab se lekar aaj tak, har din tumhare saath baat karke hi complete lagta hai.
