🌸 Promises to You

💖 I promise to never judge you, no matter what you share with me.

💖 I promise to always listen to you patiently, even when I have no solutions to give.

💖 I promise to stand by your side, whether the world is with you or not.

💖 I promise to never let you feel alone.

💖 I promise to always protect your secrets and respect your trust.

💖 I promise to motivate you when you’re low and celebrate you when you win.

💖 I promise to never fake my feelings.

💖 I promise to understand your silence too.

💖 I promise to love you in your best and worst days.

💖 I promise to choose you, every single day.

💖 I promise to never touch you without your permission.

💖 I promise to never judge you for your dressing, choices, or past.

💖 I promise to respect your decisions, even if they don’t include me.

💖 I promise to never force you for anything, ever.

💖 I promise to support your dreams as mine.

💖 I promise to make you feel safe, always.

💖 I promise to always make you smile, no matter how bad my own day is.

“Bas ek promise aur – Zindagi chahe jaisi bhi ho, mai tumhare bina to jee sakta hoon, par jeena nahi chahta.”
