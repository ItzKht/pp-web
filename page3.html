Some Special Moments 🌸

🌸 30th April – The day we met for the first time in real.
Vo chhoti si mulaqat… pehli baar aankhon se aankhein mili thi. Main nervous tha, par tumhari ek muskurahat ne sab kuch shaant kar diya. Us din laga, “Koi to hai jisse dekhkar dil ko sukoon milta hai.” 🫶🏻
🍟 6th May – Our MacD outing.
Vo kuch ghante… jab hum teeno saath baithe, baatein ki, hasse, masti ki. Jab tumne bola “You look better in real than in pictures,” us ek line ne dil ko choo liya. Us din realise hua ki ab tumhare words mere liye matter karne lage hain.
🎮 Our game calls & pranks.
Stumble Guys khelte hue, ek dusre ko chidhana, Khushi ko prank karna ki hum committed hain… un hansiyo me, un shararaton me, ek alag hi pyaar tha… ek bond jo shabd nahi samjha sakte.
💛 The day you called me your safe place.
Jab tumne apne dard, sapne, struggles sab mujhse share kiye, mujhe laga “Haan, ab vo mujhpe trust karti hai.” Tumhare har secret ne mujhe tumhare aur kareeb la diya.
🌃 Late night talks about life & fears.
Vo raaton ki baatein… jab tumhare har aansu, har khauf sunn raha tha. Ye baatein nahi, dil pe likhi hui wo kahaniyaan hain jo kabhi nahi mitengi.
🌙 8th-9th May – First Call On Your Period Pain Night.
Us raat jab tum pain me thi aur tumne bas distract hone ke liye baat karna chaha… mujhe nahi pata tha kya karu, par itna zarur tha ki mai tumhare saath tha… us call ne mujhe realise karaya ki kitna attach hogaya hoon tumse.
🎶 The song you sang for me.
Jab tumne “Pretty Little Baby” gaakar bheja… bas yunhi maine kaha tha aur tumne kar diya. Tumhari voice me itna pyaar aur comfort tha, dil bas khush hogaya tha.
💪🏻 When you complimented my shoulders.
Jab maine messy hairstyle try kiya aur tumne bola “baalon se zyada to shoulders ka pump dikh raha hai,” us din se har workout me tum yaad aati ho.
📝 The paragraph I wrote for you.
Jab maine wo lamba paragraph likha tha tumhare liye aur tumne bola tha “kabhi kisi ladke ne bina fayde ke aise likha hi nahi,” uss din laga ki shayad koi meri honesty ko samajh raha hai.
🍫 The chocolate hidden for you.
Jab tum periods me thi aur maine chocolate chhupake di thi bag me, tumhara “omg thank you” sunke dil me itna pyaar feel hua ki bas… chahta hoon hamesha tumhare liye aise hi karu.
🛡️ When you defended me.
Jab Paras aur Khushi ki fight me tumne bola “bhavya bhi mera banda hai,” uss din laga ki koi to hai jo bina kisi condition ke meri side leta hai.
😭 When you cried for your results and shared with me.
Jab tumne 12th ke result ke baad roti hui photo bheji aur maine tumhe console kiya, uss din laga ki tumhare sabse weak moment me bhi mai tumhare saath hoon.
💔 When you shared about your dad.
Jab tumne bola ki papa tumhe priority nahi dete, uss din bas dil se yahi dua nikli ki “Kaash mai tumhe vo sab de paun jo tum deserve karti ho.”
🌟 Your NEET Exam Wish.
Jab maine tumhe Neet exam se pehle lamba sa paragraph likh ke wish kiya aur tumne bola “itni preparation bhi nahi jitna aapne paragraph likh diya,” uss din laga… chahe duniya tumhe stress de, mai hamesha tumhe smile de sakta hoon.
