Our First Call

Our first proper call… mujhe ab tak yaad hai vo raat. Jab tumhe period pain ho raha tha aur tum bas distract hona chahti thi. Us raat tumhari awaaz me vo dard bhi tha aur vo innocence bhi, and I felt so helpless ki mai tumhara pain le nahi sakta. Par usi waqt mujhe realise hua, “Kitna attach hogaya hoon mai tumse…” Jab tak tum soyi nahi, I kept talking to you. Bas dil me yahi tha ki tumhe aaram mile, tumhe akela feel na ho. Us call ke baad, everything changed for me. Tumhare saath baat karna, tumhe sunna, tumhe samajhna – sab itna important lagne laga. That was the night I realised… tumhare bina subah nahi hoti aur tumhare bina raat nahi dhalti. You became my peace, my care, my comfort… and my heart’s favourite place to stay.
